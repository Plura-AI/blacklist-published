02 of 05

Jennifer Tyler

Comptroller — Contractor, both organizations

BLA PLURA
Title
Comptroller across both organizations.
Employment status
Independent contractor — paid directly by each entity (Blacklist Alliance, Verification Technologies / NV, and Plura.ai), not a W-2 employee of any.
Compensation
Each entity pays its own portion based on workload. The BLA-side entities (BLA + NV) carry the bulk of the allocation; Plura.ai pays a smaller share because the Plura accounting workload is light.

Owns the accounting function end-to-end for both organizations — bookkeeping, GL, AmEx / expense allocation, intercompany reconciliations, financial reporting, and audit support.

The current arrangement is appropriate while Plura.ai's accounting workload remains light. If Plura scales to where it warrants dedicated accounting headcount, Matt would bring in a Plura-direct accountant rather than expand Jennifer's Plura allocation.

03 of 05

Matt Davidson

Chief Marketing Officer · Both organizations

BLA PLURA Related Party
Title
CMO across both organizations.
Equity
Holds 17% of Plura.ai, held directly in his individual capacity. This interest is separate from, and not derivative of, Blacklist Alliance's position in Plura.ai (BLA holds 75.5%). Matt Davidson holds no equity in Blacklist Alliance or Verification Technologies.
What he does
Owns the marketing function across both organizations — built and maintains the Blacklist Alliance and Number Verifier websites, manages all brand sites, writes ad copy, designs and produces trade show booths, and runs creative and paid media.
Comp structure
(Proposed restructure)
One retainer with Blacklist Alliance covering both Blacklist Alliance and Number Verifier websites and web creative work, plus a separate arrangement with Plura.ai. Rationale: covering both organizations is effectively a full-time job; a shared-services structure is more cost-effective than pulling him fully onto Plura while still needing equivalent senior marketing leadership at BLA / NV. This is a proposed restructure of the current arrangement.
Reporting line
Reports to Matt Beucler across both organizations.

Diligence note · Internal only

Matt Davidson is both a related party to Plura.ai (individual equityholder) and a service provider to both organizations. Polsinelli to frame the related-party disclosure before externalizing — confirm that services to BLA and NV under the retainer are documented as arm's-length and independent of his Plura.ai equity position.

04 of 05

Kristyn Outlaw

Sales Associate · Cross-Organization

BLA PLURA
Title
Sales Associate. Trained on and authorized to sell across both organizations (Plura.ai, Number Verifier, Blacklist Alliance).
Employment status
Independent contractor. Holds three separate Independent Contractor Agreements — one with Plura.ai, one with Number Verifier, and one with Blacklist Alliance. Each agreement carries its own commission schedule. She is paid directly by each entity, not through a single payor with cross-billing.
Lead context
All assigned / inbound lead flow at the company is currently directed to Plura.ai. BLA-side (NV and Blacklist) new business is driven through outbound effort, referrals, and cross-sell from existing accounts.
Scope
  • New sales focus: Plura.ai. The product she most enjoys selling and the strongest fit for her skill set.
  • Existing client management: Maintains her existing client books across both organizations. No plan to take these accounts away from her.
  • Cross-sell: Authorized and encouraged to cross-sell Plura clients into Number Verifier and Blacklist Alliance.
Compensation
Paid on the life of the client per the commission schedule in each respective ICA — trailing commissions / residuals on the accounts she has brought in and continues to manage. Comp obligations sit with the entity that owns each underlying client relationship.
Forward plan
Same structure. Plura-primary for new sales, continued management of and trailing comp on her existing BLA / NV books, and ongoing cross-sell of Plura clients into NV and BLA.
Reporting line
Reports to Jesse Mullen (VP of Sales, Plura.ai) on new business; remains supported by Esteban Garcia (VP of Sales, BLA / NV) on her existing BLA-side book.

Diligence note · Internal only

Life-of-client commission obligations on her BLA / NV book should be schedule-disclosed as ongoing commission liabilities of the respective entities. The three separate ICAs themselves should be included in the data room.

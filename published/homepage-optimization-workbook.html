Homepage rebuild for the campaigns that point here

Every live search campaign except the blog/SKAG test drives to this one homepage: Brand, General (TCPA + DNC + litigator scrub), Verticals (Contact Centers, VoIP, Insurance, Digital Marketers, AI), and Competitors (DNC.com, Gryphon, PossibleNOW). With no dedicated landers, this page has to earn the Quality Score for all of them.

The core problem

The current H1 — "Risk less. Connect more." — is a brand tagline with zero keyword relevance. Google's Quality Score rewards the landing page reflecting the search term in its visible headline and copy. Over all of 2026, "tcpa" dominates impressions while DNC scrubbing drives the most non-brand clicks (469), with litigator scrub and STIR/SHAKEN close behind — almost none of which appear above the fold today.

The fix (and how we keep Seth's tagline)

Keep "risk less. connect more." as the lowercase brand line — it stays on the page and can still carry the trademark. Put a keyword-rich H1 directly beneath it. That's actually the brand's own signature pattern: lowercase tagline + a bigger headline. We get the trademark and the relevance. Below are the H1/H2 options, a block-by-block rewrite, the keyword gaps, and an FAQ section — then flip to Preview to see it built.

Flag for Matt — not page copy, campaign hygiene

1. "Free" intent. free dnc scrub, free dnc checker, free dnc list, free litigator scrub — meaningful impressions, ~0 clicks and 0 conversions. Tire-kickers. Add as negatives rather than writing "free" copy.

What is the TCPA?
The Telephone Consumer Protection Act is the federal law governing calls and texts to consumers. Violations run $500–$1,500 per call or message, which is why a single campaign can turn into a class action. The Blacklist Alliance helps you stay on the right side of it.
What's a litigator scrub?
Before you dial, we check each number against our proprietary database of known TCPA litigators and serial plaintiffs and flag the risky ones — so you don't call the people most likely to sue.
Do you scrub state do-not-call lists too?
Yes. We scrub the federal DNC registry plus all state lists, and we track state mini-TCPA rules like Florida's FTSA and the stricter California requirements.
Is the scrub real-time?
Yes. Use our API for real-time scrubbing inside your dialer or platform, or run lists in batch. Most teams are integrated in days, not weeks.
How is this different from DNC.com, Gryphon, or PossibleNOW?
One platform covering litigator scrubbing, DNC and state lists, a litigation firewall, and caller-ID reputation — backed by a proprietary litigator database and used by roughly 1,000 clients in the calling space.
Can I see it first?
Yes — book a free demo and we'll show you the Litigation Firewall against your own call flow.
